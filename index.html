Loft

The loft style is a popular choice for interior design because it combines industrial and urban elements with a touch of modernity. Exposed brick walls, rustic wood beams, and metal fixtures all contribute to the rugged, industrial look of a loft space. However, the use of minimalist decor and clean, modern lines helps to balance out the raw, edgy feel of the space. Many loft interiors also incorporate vintage and eclectic pieces, creating a unique and artfully designed space.

Modern

Modern interior design is characterized by sleek lines, minimalism, and a focus on function. It is a style that values simplicity and cleanliness above all else, often incorporating monochromatic color schemes and a limited number of decorative elements. Modern interiors often use a combination of natural materials, such as wood and stone, along with man-made materials like metal and glass.

A tabbed interface or simply a tab is a graphical control element one can use to contain multiple panels or documents onto a single window for users to access. Because of this, it is perfect for single page web pages and applications. It gives users a neat and organized UI and makes the navigation process a whole lot easier.
